TEMB 3201 · Group Project · In Progress · 2026
Adaptive Motor-Imagery BCI Using Riemannian Geometry (A-MDRM System)
Kyambogo University · Dept. of Mechatronics & Biomedical Engineering · Supervisor: Mr. Bagaiga Richard · Team: Among Mary Shila, Econgu Emmanuel, Marvin Ngabonzira
Developing a session-adaptive EEG Motor Imagery classification system leveraging Riemannian geometry on covariance matrices for subject-independent decoding. Implements MOABB's BCI Competition IV Dataset 2a pipeline with hardware-in-the-loop validation via Arduino. The system addresses session-to-session variability — one of the primary barriers to real-world BCI deployment.
